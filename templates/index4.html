Lena Hartmann

PhD Researcher · Westgate University

I am a fourth-year PhD student at Westgate University, supervised by Prof. Daniel Yuen, and co-supervised by Prof. Fatima Osei. Previously, I earned my MSc from the University of Avenmark with Prof. Riku Saarinen and my BEng from Celadon Institute of Technology. My research focuses on the safety, fairness, and robustness of large language models and AI-driven decision systems.

I am currently on the job market and open to postdoctoral positions in AI safety, AI fairness, and trustworthy machine learning.
